How It Works

  1. Sign up and receive your own simple, easy-to-remember Ajent email address.
  2. Move your existing email subscriptions to your Ajent account.
  3. Visit Ajent to easily manage your consumer related email traffic.
  4. Share your favorite things with your friends!

What You Can Do With Ajent

Manage Your Subscriptions:

  • UNSUBSCRIBE from things you've been getting that you don't want. When you sign up for Ajent, you can immediately do this at the click of a button.
  • HIDE emails you might want to get at some point but rarely need to see. Ajent allows you to turn email subscriptions "on" and "off" at your convenience. No need to unsubscribe and re-subscribe, again, it is just a click of a button!
  • BUNDLE emails that you want to review all at once. For instance, you can go through all of your Daily Candy emails once a week â€“ instead of shuffling through them every day.

Keep Track of Particular Emails You Need:

  • SET expiration dates for daily deals, set event dates for travel emails, and use Ajent alerts to notify you when dates come up.
  • SAVE important receipts, itineraries and other crucial emails in one secure place, so you don't have to weed through your personal inbox to find them.
  • CREATE folders to keep track of your personal interests and different types of email subscriptions.

Share and Learn:

  • FOLLOW what your friends and other Ajent users are finding, wanting and buying. Have a friend who always has the best shoes? Want to know where wine lovers get their inside information? Find them on Ajent.
  • SHARE with your friends when you come across something fantastic. It is easy to post to your friends and the Ajent community — right from your inbox or from your share page.
