FAQ

What is Ajent?

Ajent is the SMART email subscription service that simplifies your life by helping you better sort, manage and share messages from daily deals, retailers, travel sites and blogs. Ajent keeps your inbox free so that you never have to give out your personal email address to a newsletter, travel service or store again. At the same time, it allows you to see what your friends are interested in, share your own interests with others, and manage all of your email subscriptions in one easy place.

How does Ajent work?

  1. Sign up and receive your own simple, easy-to-remember Ajent email address.
  2. Move your existing email subscriptions to your Ajent account.
  3. Visit Ajent to easily manage your consumer related email traffic.
  4. Share your favorite things with your friends!

How do I sign up?

Visit our Sign Up page, where all we need is your name and basic email account information to get started. We’ll connect your email account to your Ajent account so that your email subscriptions go directly to Ajent from now on. Then we’ll give you an easy-to-remember Ajent email address so you won’t have to give out your personal address the next time a store or company asks you for your email.

How long will it take me to sign up?

Signing up is quick and painless. It only takes a moment to create your Ajent account. It takes a few more moments for us to scan your email subscriptions and verify which ones should come to Ajent. You can choose to complete this process now, or finish it later.

What if I lose my Ajent password or forget my email address?

Simply visit our Help page and we’ll be happy to reconnect you with your Ajent account.
